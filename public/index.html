Welcome to ApexBudget!

Let's take a quick 1-minute tour to set up your wallet.

Step 1 of 5

🚀 Command Center

ApexBudget helps you balance studies and finances. Track your Current Balance, Allowances/Income, and Monthly Spent card values in real-time.

Key Tip: Maintain a high Savings Rate to automatically build tuition and textbook buffers.

🎯 Target Spending Limit

Set a monthly spending threshold to curb impulse buys. The radial progress bar warns you when you exhaust 80% of your target, and glows red when exceeded.

Configurable: Tap the "Adjust Monthly Budget" link on the radial widget to scale limit settings anytime.

🍔 Smooth Category Selection

Categorize expenses like Academics, Utilities, or Commute. The interactive category selector simplifies classification, while real-time charts summarize ratios.

Side Hustles: Toggle the type selector to "Income" to log peer tutoring or allowance checks directly.

📊 Financial Ledger & Excel

Search, filter, edit, or delete transactions in the Financial Ledger. Need physical reports? Click "Export Excel" to download formatted spreadsheets with formulas.

Exchange Rates: Excel files embed dynamic lookup tables that calculate values based on your chosen currency.

🌐 Multi-Currency & Live Rates

ApexBudget fetches live rates for USD, EUR, GBP, INR, JPY, and CAD. Swapping currency converts all charts, limits, and histories instantly.

All Done! You're set to master your college finance journey. Tap "Get Started" to enter.
